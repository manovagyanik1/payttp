How it works

PayTTP is a trusted party which acts as a mediator between buyer and seller. In cases where buyer and seller don't trust each other PayTTP is there to help them do the transaction. Consider PayTTP as the medium in which the buyer and seller communicates. Whenever the buyer and seller want to transact, they create a group including buyer, seller and PayTTP. The buyer transfers the item price to PayTTP instead of the seller, then PayTTP informs the seller to provide the service to the buyer. Once seller does so, PayTTP asks for the acknowledgement from the buyer, once received the money is transferred to the seller. If not a conflict case is created which is then solved the PayTTP's support team.

Story behind PayTTP

We(the founders) went to a new city for some work and few services were promised to them by a vendor. We transferred advance money to the vendor, but the vendor was no where at the promised date to provide the service. Even after multiple attempts to reach the vendor, we couldn't reach him. We were ripped. And this wasn't the first time it was happening.

After a lot of explorations we got to know that a lot of people all over the globe are suffering from some or the other form of non-trustworthines and there is no geneuine solution for the same. That was the eureka moment!!
