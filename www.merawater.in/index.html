Frequently Asked Questions

What is the offer detail?

Refer your friend and earn equal number of Quality Water bottles for free. Your and your friend's address whom you are referring can't be same, and you can't refer our already existing customers. Once your referred friend orders from us, we will update your account with equal number of Quality Water bottles which you can order any later time. Limited period offer!!! Pizza offer is closed now.

Do I need to pay online?

You don't need to pay online, you can give the money at the time of delivery.

How are we providing these offers?

We are in association with couponshub.in ,our users will be showered with all these offers in coming future.

What areas do we cover?

We are trying to cover as much bangalore as we can but currently we can assure delivery for the one mentioned in the order form dropdown. But you can place order for your location selecting other as locality, we will reach you if we can, if we can't we will try to extend our services in future for your locality.

What is unique customer Id?

This is a newly introduced feature incorporating the feedbacks from customers. Whenever a customer orders a unique Id is generated for each customer based on mobile and email, so at next order time you don't need to fill the entire form again, just enter your unique Id and you are done.

Do we provide other brands of water can?

Once you are done with your order form, we call you back to confirm your order. In case you want other brands water bottle like Bislery, Kinley just tell us in the call we will deliver it to you. Price we take to deliver Bislery bottle is Rs 75/-.

I don't have any empty can with me, Do I need to give can deposit?

Yes, we charge Rs 150/- as the can deposit. But whenever you want to stop our service you can return us our can and take your deposit back.

Do I need to order 6 or 12 water all at once to avail Flexi or Family offer?

No, you can order water can one by one and once your order count reaches 6 we will give you the next can for free, and once it reaches 12 you get a total of 3 cans for free.

Is their some special plans for bulk delivery or businesses?

Yes, we do provide additional benefits to businesses and places where we provide bulk orders. But for that you have to contact us, call us or send a mail here.
